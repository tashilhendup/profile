ABOUT ME

-Why Me

Working as a -Fronted Web-Developer -


I've spent the last TWO years working on Upwork. Prior to that, I worked for a number of small IT firms in the area. My Upwork job has been fantastic from the start. Clear communication, I feel, is the key to my success here. So far, I've worked on over 150+ projects and have a 100% job success rate. I enjoy starting enterprises from the ground up and putting things together as cleanly as possible. Working with organizations that have a well-established presence is simple, but it lacks the challenges that come with managing something from the ground up.
